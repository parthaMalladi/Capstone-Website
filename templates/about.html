Why I Created Health Finder

I want to create a website that offers users health analytics based on their own health markers. A machine learning algorithm trained on pre-existing datasets will predict the risk of various ailments based on the user’s inputs. My project’s goal is to improve people’s health outcomes by educating people about their health. The website will ask the user for various markers such as age, weight, blood pressure, cholesterol, and so on.
