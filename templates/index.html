What We Offer

Heart Disease

Heart disease refers to a range of conditions that affect the heart, most commonly involving narrowed or blocked blood vessels that can lead to heart attacks, chest pain, or stroke. It’s often caused by a buildup of plaque in the arteries and is influenced by factors like high blood pressure, smoking, and high cholesterol.

Stroke

A stroke occurs when blood flow to part of the brain is blocked or a blood vessel in the brain bursts, causing brain cells to die. It can lead to lasting brain damage, disability, or even death if not treated quickly.

Type 2 Diabetes

Type 2 diabetes is a chronic condition where the body becomes resistant to insulin or doesn't produce enough of it, leading to high blood sugar levels. It’s often linked to lifestyle factors like poor diet, lack of exercise, and obesity, but genetics can also play a role.
